About us
JSC VIKEDA (Joint-Stock Company VIKEDA) is the newest and the most modern ice cream production company in Lithuania that started its production activity on the 12th of December 1997. During the period of its activity, the company became the most known and progressive one, and it presents novelties to Lithuanian and foreign gourmands every year. The modern and flexible production equipment, the high skill of workers and good organization of work allow us to produce such products that satisfy needs of all consumers. The progress with the Food Safety System HACCP gives a large opportunities to work worldwide.
